# Privacy Policy — WhatsApp Group ID Reveal

**Last updated: May 7, 2026**

## What this extension does

WhatsApp Group ID Reveal reads group metadata from WhatsApp Web's local IndexedDB storage (stored in your own browser) and displays each group's JID as a badge in the chat list. When you click a badge, the JID is copied to your clipboard.

## Data collection

This extension does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any user data. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

## Permissions

The extension requires access to `web.whatsapp.com` solely to inject the UI badge into the WhatsApp Web chat list. It does not access any other websites.
